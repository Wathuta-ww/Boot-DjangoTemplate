Solar Energy System

A solar power system comprises several key components working in tandem to harness sunlight and convert it into usable electricity. At its core are solar panels, composed of photovoltaic cells that capture sunlight and initiate the electricity generation process.

Inverters play a vital role by converting the direct current (DC) electricity generated by the solar panels into alternating current (AC), which is compatible with most household appliances and the electric grid.

Additionally, depending on the system's design and requirements, batteries or grid connections may be included to store excess energy for use during periods of low sunlight or to feed surplus energy back into the grid, respectively.

We offer state of the art solar panels with a range of power rating, inverters and solar batteries. Their fit for any type of home all you have to do is ask for our expert opinion and you are set.
